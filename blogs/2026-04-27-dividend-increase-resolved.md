At the Annual General Meeting of Henkel AG & Co. KGaA on April 27, 2026, the shareholders approved all agenda items. A total of about 87 percent of the voting capital stock was represented. As in the previous year, the Annual General Meeting was held as an in-person event at the CCD-Stadthalle in Düsseldorf.
